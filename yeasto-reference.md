# Yeasto Website Reference

**URL:** https://yeasto.app/

**Date Saved:** 2025

## Overview

Yeasto is a professional dough calculator app for pizza and bread. The website showcases scientific precision meets intuitive design for consistent, bakery-quality results at home.

## Key Features Highlighted

### Scientific Precision
- Q10 temperature modeling
- Flour strength analysis
- Professional calculators
- Salt activity adjustment

### Live Tracking
- Live Activities & Dynamic Island
- Multi-stage fermentation tracking
- Environment adaptation (WeatherKit & HomeKit)
- Smart notifications

### Recipe Management
- Custom recipes (unlimited with Premium)
- 15+ professional templates
- Recipe builder with guided creation
- Baker's percentages
- CloudKit sync

### Analytics
- 5-star rating system
- Multi-timeframe analytics (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, all-time)
- Interactive charts
- AI-powered insights
- Correlation analysis
- Coach insights
